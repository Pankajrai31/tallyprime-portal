Run Tally Prime from anywhere.

Securely access Tally Prime in your browser as a Remote App on Azure Virtual Desktop. Sign in with your work account and your Tally session opens in a new tab — no install required.

Browser based

Launch Tally Prime in any modern browser. Nothing to install on the user's device.

Bring your own license

Activate your Tally license inside the session — your data, your license, your control.

Secure by design

Identity is enforced with Microsoft Entra ID. Sessions run on Azure Virtual Desktop in India.

How it works

  1. Click Sign in and use your work or school account.
  2. Click Open Tally Prime — a new tab loads the Azure Virtual Desktop client.
  3. Pick the Tally Prime app tile and start working. Activate your Tally license on first launch.
